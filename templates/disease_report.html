ℹ️ How it works

1. Upload a clear photo of the affected crop
2. Select the crop type and describe the problem
3. Our agricultural experts will review and provide recommendations
4. Receive medicine suggestions and treatment advice

📷

Click or Drag to Upload Photo

Maximum file size: 16MB

Supported: JPG, PNG, JPEG, GIF

📋 Tips for Better Diagnosis

  • Take photos in good lighting (natural daylight is best)
  • Capture close-up images showing affected areas clearly
  • Include multiple photos if disease appears in different parts
  • Describe when you first noticed the problem
  • Mention any recent weather changes or treatments applied
  • Note if neighboring crops are also affected
